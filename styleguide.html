A generative manifest in evolutionon

Principles for inner ecoology!

Natural gift Authentic giving and reciving

Gifting does not contemplate an expectation, a return or an exchange for somehting of equal value. It rather creates connection and trust and a conscious awareness about the values and the responsibility of what we give and receive. We want to learn to disattach from ownership - to let artifacts, land, knowledge and ressources free from ownership and rather integrate them communal.

Radical integration Autonomy, Reliance, Commitment

Our community is committed to a radically participatory ethic. We encourage people to become active participants in the design process, embracing ideas of conviviality and exchange to foster social accountability and enhance communities.

Vulnerability Mutual support and trust building

We celebrate the state of failing as an act of self-acceptance and connection with reality. Allowing ourselves to be seen in a moment of vulnerability is the first step for making mutual support and trust possible. Fear, pain and our limits in general has to become visible in order to be changed. We want to create an environment that allows us to enjoy and embrace transformation.
