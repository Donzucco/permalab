Attitude & Principles

We are aware that our inner landscape is designing our outer landscape. We want to find the right questions to be able to focus on the right solutions. Here we want to design and use tools to get to the bottom of conflict resolution, trust building, contact and awarness in relationschips and nature.

Gif economy & Support

Startig by creating it around children we want to cocreate with nature a sustainable ecosystem and infrastructure that inhance community and the use of our collective intelligence. Stepping out of ownership and the story of separation we want to focus on making visible what connect us and in wich way we can integrate the gifts that life and the planet is providing to us.
