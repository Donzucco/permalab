A laboratory for sustainable living solutions

We design a research center and living place in Portugal made alive by a group of pioneers, adventurers, activists and dreamers.

We create a sustainable valley in south portugal with the aim to be a base for evolutionary work. We commit to put our abilities, time and energy in co-creating with nature a living place wehre we can focus of aswering the questions: how can we live togehter in order to connect with our individual and collective potentials, and how can we do it in cooperation with nature?

We wish to see this place as a save space for experiments, retreats, education and a place wehre children can grow in deep contact with nature and people.

The model

We have to design new structures if we want to step out of the old ones. The change basically happens in our inner! There are no ready recipes for building new ways of living. However, working on a model is a strategy for detaching from our personal history and habits and connect with a more holistic way to live. Designing our reality from scratch wants to be the process of rediscovering our deepest desires and potentials.

The collective

The Land

We decide to find land in portugal. It is a region that is strong connected with the natural forces of water, sun, air and earth. It is also far away from industrial, commercial centers and describe the line between desertification and potential paradise. Healing the land from the old mechanism of exploitation is our inspiration for inner work and the best way to reconnect with nature.
