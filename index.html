Ecovillage Design in Portugal

A creative laboratory for sustainable models in Portugal - Alentejo made alive by a group of pioneers, adventurers, activists and dreamers.

We are designing a bridge between city and land, crowded and vast, between integration and retreat. From the urban jungle, we want to build a landing place in Portugal that allows us to breath and to reconnect deeply with what really sacred to us.

The model

We have to design new structures if we want to step out of the old ones. The change basically happens in our inner! There are no ready recipes for building new ways of living. However, working on a model is a strategy for detaching from our personal history and habits. Designing our reality from scratch wants to be the the process of rediscovering our deepest desires and potentials.

Research Center

We are aware that our inner landscape is designing our outer landscape. We want to find the right questions to be able to focus on the right solutions. Here we want to design and use tools to get to the bottom of conflict resolution, trust building, contact and awarness in relationschips and communication and see nature as our teacher and supporter.

Living Place
