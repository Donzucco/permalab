A generative manifest in evolutionon

Principles for inner ecoology!

Natural gift Authentic giving and reciving

We see gifting as an act of became aware about our ability to give and recive and the recollocation of our values into the metodology of exchanging resources and energy. Giving: The act of giving is coming from a place of abbondancy in us.Is is the natural act of supporting energy flow like plants do. It is the economy of ecosystems. Gifting means to ask to yourself . What do i have that i can give and what are my gifts to the world? it also means reevaluating the truhly experession of yourself. The reciver: the act of reciving is basicly the act of recognizing and welcoming the unknow, unexpected gifts that live are bring to you. It means accepting Gifteconomy circle circles are small models of sociaty! In a gifting circles you make visible what your need are and what you have to give. In this space you experience the hidden power and potentials of human connections.

Radical integration Autonomy, Reliance, Commitment

Our community is committed to a radically participatory ethic. We encourage people to become active participants in the design process, embracing ideas of conviviality and exchange to foster social accountability and enhance communities.

Vulnerability Mutual support and trust building

We celebrate the state of failing as an act of self-acceptance and connection with reality. Allowing ourselves to be seen in a moment of vulnerability is the first step for making mutual support and trust possible. Fear, pain and our limits in general has to become visible in order to be changed. We want to create an environment that allows us to enjoy and embrace transformation.
